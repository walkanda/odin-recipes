A delicious Fried Chicken Recipe

Enjoy this delicious fried chicken recipe passed down from grandma

e sure to get all th correct ingredients shown below

ingredients

  • Chicken drumsticks
  • Flour
  • seasonings
  • Frying oil or lard

Steps

  1. Wash and dry chicken
  2. Combine flour and seasonings
  3. Cover chicken in flour and seasonings
  4. Fry at 350 degrees
  5. Let it sit for 5 minutes before serving
